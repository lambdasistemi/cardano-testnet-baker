  > Replaces the test-only synthesis runner stub with `dbSynthesizerRunner`,
  > which invokes the stock upstream `db-synthesizer` binary via

  > `db-synthesizer` is on `PATH` for any `nix run` consumer. Each

  > slot/time math is deterministic without leaking that timestamp

  > `securityParam` from 10 to 2 because the upstream synthesizer
  > rejects the previous combination at this `epochLength`.
  > `nix/checks.nix` and `just CI` switch to a single synthesized
  > `local-fast` round-trip; `acceptance-normal` is dropped from the
  > local CI recipe (see follow-up below).
